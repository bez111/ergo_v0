# Community Guidelines

Universal principles for an open, safe, and productive Ergo community.

- Respect and kindness to all members
- No hate speech or personal attacks
- Be open-minded; accept diverse ideas
- Critique constructively, never as an attack
- No spamming or self-promotion without permission
- Verify news before sharing — no misinformation
- No price manipulation or pump/dump shilling
- Zero tolerance for scams and phishing
- Never share private keys or sensitive info
- Use best security practices at all times
- Moderators may ban/restrict for violations

Enjoy learning, building, and connecting in the Ergo ecosystem!
